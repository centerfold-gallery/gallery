During this week, we'll be showcasing the work of Andrew Nowacki.

Andrew Nowacki is a self-taught photographer who uses his keen eye for detail and line to produce gorgeous images, often showcasing overlooked geometry found in mundane environments. As opposed to a stark mathematical tone, however, Andrew manipulates light and shadow to exude a measured sense of warmth. In addition to his work as a photographer, Andrew co-runs Inversions Collective: a not-for-profit group that fosters opportunity for up-and-coming artists within the industry.
