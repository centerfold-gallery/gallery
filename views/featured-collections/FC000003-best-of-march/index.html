For the month of March, we opened our webspace to women, People of Colour, Indigenous peoples, and queer, trans and non-binary individuals in an effort to encourage people who possess these identities to share their work with us and use our space to promote themselves and their practice.

This featured collection, entitled, Best of March 2018, showcases some of the work we received. The variety in the works, across media and subject matter, represent the diversity we were happy to celebrate last month, and every month, for as long as we can.
