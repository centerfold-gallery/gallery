Parts of a Whole is a collection of work that explores the collage as a medium and art-form. By definition, collage is a process by which differing forms are assembled to create a new whole. Unrelated media from digital or physical sources are compiled and ordered to create a brand new image—one that purveys a meaning not found in the sum of its parts.
As with the media used in each collage, these works are from different sources, assembled into the meta-collage that is this collection.

We hope you enjoy it.
