Marketplace — General
What is Centerfold?
Centerfold is a Montreal-based organization with the aim of driving social and economic change in the art industry. Our goal is to enable artists to gain funding, visibility, and access while increasing art awareness within the community.
Marketplace — Artists
I'm interested in signing up for Centerfold's online marketplace to show and sell my work, how do I do that?
We have a submission form that you can access through the website in order to submit to Centerfold's Marketplace.

You can also submit directly here.
I'm an artist, how do I get featured in one of your Centerfold Series Exhibitions?
For questions related to the Centerfold Series, see here.
Can I use Centerfold's logo to promote my artwork being featured on the site?
Yes! We really encourage this. Essentially, the more you promote to your friends, family, and followers, the more likely someone will buy your artwork.

You can find assets to share on your different social media platforms here.
Does Centerfold charge commission on artworks sold through the Marketplace?
We charge a 25% commission on any sale on the Centerfold Marketplace.

We don't charge any commission on sales of artworks that are currently being featured in a Centerfold Series Exhibition. You can learn more happens with money from sales of Centerfold Series Exhibition artworks here.
Filing taxes
Due to different tax laws and obligations across provinces, states, and countries, we are not able to give advice on filing taxes. We recommend that you reach out to a tax professional in your area if you have any questions regarding your potential tax obligations from any money you make from Centerfold.
What are my responsibilities as an artist who's showing and selling work on the Centerfold Marketplace?
Here's a list of your responsibilities:

• Only submit work that has been created by you. We have a strict policy on stolen work. Also, just don't steal, it's not cool. We take this very seriously.
• Provide accurate information on the artwork (condition, dimensions, materials, etc.)
• Inform Centerfold of any change in the condition of the artwork (for example, if the artwork was damaged or scratched)
• Maintain prompt communication with us in case of a sale
• Promptly ship the artwork to a buyer, making sure to properly package the work before sending it.
• Promote not just yourself, but the other artists in the exhibition.

Marketplace — Artists — Receiving Payments
How do I get paid? What are my options for getting paid?
We will send your payment via Interac E-Transfer, wire transfer, or PayPal once the funds are transferred into our account. The funds generally take around 1 week to process. Once they have been processed, your payments will be issued. Based on the way you are getting paid, this timeline varies, but you will get out ASAP!
How do I set up my pay-out information? How is this and my personal information stored?
We'll reach out to you to collect this information once one of your artworks sells.

Your information will be stored offline, on a password protected folder, and will be immediately deleted once your funds have been transferred. It will never, ever, be shared with anyone because we don't roll like that. If you would like more information about privacy on Centerfold, you can read about it here.
Marketplace — Buyers
I just bought an artwork, what can I expect?
Great! Thank you for supporting one of the artists on the platform.

Once you purchase an artwork, we'll send you an email with a receipt and to notify you of the current location of the artwork and as estimated processing timeline. Once the artwork is shipped, we'll email you with the tracking number.

Please allow up to 5 business days to prepare the artwork to ship. Shipping timelines vary on source and final destination, but we'll be in touch with you every step of the way.
How is shipping calculated?
Right now shipping costs vary between $15 and $175 USD depending on the value and dimensions of the artwork. This ensures we can deliver your artwork to you safely, wherever you are in the world. We will keep the information about shipping here, and on individual artwork pages, updated based on any changes that we may have to make for extremely large (or small) works.

If you have any questions about shipping please don't hesitate to get in contact with us at support@centerfold.gallery.
Can I negotiate the price of an artwork?
Prices listed on the website are final. If you're eyeing an artwork and it's just above your budget, you can sign up for our mailing list to be informed of any promotions or sales we might be running.

You can sign up here
How do I ensure the authenticity of an artwork?
You will receive a Certificate of Authenticity with every purchase of an artwork. You should hold on to this certificate, and store it in a safe place in case you choose to re-sell the artwork at a later date. We do not conform to archiac unspoken rules like other galleries. You are more than welcome to sell an artwork you've bought from us, without asking us. It's yours, and you can do what you want with it.
I am hesitant to buy an artwork without seeing it in person. Can I schedule a viewing?
Seeing and experiencing an artwork in person can be a decisive step in the acquisition process. In most cases, we are happy to coordinate a visit for you to view an artwork in person, in Montreal and Toronto. In some cases (such as when the artist has elected to hold and ship the artwork themselves), we may be able to schedule a studio visit for you. Please email us as support@centerfold.gallery to schedule a viewing.

For prospective buyers located outside of Montreal and Toronto, we would happy to provide you with hi-resolution images and/or videos of artworks under consideration in order to better visualize the piece. If you would like to send us a picture of a space you invision putting your artwork in, please let us know, and we will send you a mockup of the artwork, to scale, in your space. We are working on an AR app to better facilitate this process in the future.

To request hi-resolution images, videos, or a mockup, please send us an email at support@centerfold.gallery.
Can I return the work if I do not feel as strongly about it when it arrives?
Unfortunately, once a sale is made, we are unable to provide a refund.
I received a damaged artwork, what do I do?
First of all, allow us to apologize for any damage that might have occurred while shipping.

Please get in touch with us immediately at support@centerfold.gallery.
